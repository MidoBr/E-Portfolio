e-portfolio Artifact 1

Research proposal presentation – Outline

The objective of this presentation as mentioned by the university of Essex online is to “Evaluate critically existing literature, research design and methodology for your chosen topic, including data analysis processes and so produce and evaluate critically a research proposal for your chosen topic”.
Word count: 1500 – 2250.
Video duration: 15 minutes.
It is required to create a transcript in which you will present the following sections:

Project Title.

Secure cloud computing: for building effective healthcare information system

Significance/Contribution to the discipline/Research Problem.

A discussion on how to implement security measures to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information systems. Furthermore, the research aims to illustrate the significance of setting security standards and control to minimize the cyber-attacks landscape.

Research Question.

Evaluate the resilience of cloud computing technologies against sophisticated cyberattacks and demonstrate why healthcare providers should adopt cloud computing to store and manage their sensitive data.

Aims and Objectives.

The research aims to address the challenges of ensuring data security in cloud-based healthcare systems while also emphasizing the importance of effectiveness in delivering healthcare services.

Key literature related to the project.
Methodology/Development strategy/Research Design.

The research design aims to explore the security reasons that motivate healthcare providers to acquire cloud computing, as well as investigating the effectiveness of this technology for building secure information systems. Furthermore, the intention is to apply a mixed method research in which qualitative data could support the how and why should healthcare providers adopt cloud computing, and to what extent this technology is beneficial could be supported by quantitative approach.

Ethical considerations and risk assessment (as part of your ethical approval application).

Discuss and elaborate how sensitive data of patients are protected, and what measures are used to maintain the privacy of patients. Moreover, exploring the risks associated with adoption of cloud computing and how to manage it.
