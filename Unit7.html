Initial Post

To conduct a research, ethical guidelines emphasize the importance of integrity, and transparency. In this case, Abi has an ethical obligation to report his findings accurately and without bias to Whizzz. This includes presenting both positive and negative statistical analyses when applicable (CLD2, 2023).

To emphasize the significance of integrity and honesty in statistical reporting, the guidelines for statistical practices have been published by the American statistical association (ASA). The Ethical Guidelines published by the ASA for Statistical Practice indicates that statisticians should "promote and practice transparency, honesty, and integrity in their professional activities, particularly in reporting results." With that being said, it is essential that Abi provides a balanced representation of results and avoids selective reporting (ASA, 2022).

Similarly, guidelines for ethical publishing were introduced by the committee of publication ethics (COPE). COPE emphasizes how it is important for researchers to present accurate findings, and the significance of not being biased (COPE, 2022).

In conclusion, based on the principles and ethical guidelines of research integrity, Abi would be obligated to introduce both the positive and negative analyses to provide an unbiased and comprehensive assessment of the nutritional value of Whizzz. This part of his due diligence ensures transparency and transfers the responsibility to the manufacturing by allowing them to make an informed decision based on all available information.
